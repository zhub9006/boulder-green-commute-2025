# Boulder Green Commute Guide

A sustainable travel guide for Boulder residents — walking and biking routes to help you commute smarter, healthier, and greener.

### Turn-by-Turn Walking Directions

1. Head south on Oak Avenue (97 m)
2. Turn right onto 19th Street (2.16 km - main neighborhood stretch)
3. Turn left onto 20th Street (106 m)
4. Continue on 20th Street (566 m)
5. Turn right onto Pearl Street (216 m - downtown Boulder pedestrian core)
6. Turn left onto 18th Street (249 m)
7. Continue onto Canyon Boulevard (637 m - arrive at 1800 Broadway)

### Route Highlights

- **Pearl Street Mall** - Iconic downtown Boulder pedestrian zone with shops, cafes, and street performers
- **Canyon Boulevard** - Scenic path along Boulder Creek with views of the Flatirons
- Well-maintained sidewalks and crosswalks throughout

## Tips for Boulder Commuters

### Walking
- **Weather-ready:** Boulder sun is strong - bring sunscreen and a hat even in winter
- **Trail advantage:** In winter, the Canyon Boulevard path is one of the first to be cleared
- **Group walking:** Join or start a walking pool with coworkers for safety and motivation

### Biking
- **Year-round biking:** Many Boulder residents bike year-round - invest in good lights and winter tires
- **Lock up safely:** Use a U-lock and secure your bike to designated bike racks, especially downtown
- **Play it safe on Pearl:** Dismount and walk your bike through the Pearl Street Mall pedestrian zone
- **Check the path:** Boulder Creek path conditions can change - Boulder Open Space and Mountain Parks posts trail updates

### General
- **Combine with transit:** RTD buses loop through downtown Boulder - bike + bus combos extend your range
- **Commute challenges:** Join the Boulder Climate Action Plan for incentives and challenges
- **Share the road:** Boulder is a Bike Friendly Community - be respectful of all road users
